## Recommended Specifications:
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **Dimensions:** 1920x1080px or higher (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **File Size:** Optimized for web (under 500KB)
- **Content:** High-quality image of your hotel exterior, lobby, or signature space
- **Orientation:** Landscape
- **Quality:** High resolution, professionally shot

## Image Tips:
- Use a dramatic, eye-catching image that represents your luxury brand
- Ensure good contrast so white text is readable
- Consider the composition - leave space in the center for overlaid text
- Optimize the image before uploading to improve page load speed
